What are Infinity Roses?

What are Infinity Roses?

Infinity roses, also known as "eternal rose" or "eternal roses", are an extraordinary wonder of flower preservation. These roses are distinguished by their beauty and longevity. But what exactly is an Infinity Rose and how does it differ from a “normal” rose from the flower shop?

It is important to understand that Infinity roses are real roses. They are picked at the optimal time of flowering and treated through a special preservation process that preserves their natural beauty for several years. This process involves carefully removing their natural juice and replacing it with a mixture of glycerin and other preservatives. This process preserves the shape, color and even some of the natural feel of the rose petals.

The first Infinity roses were created in France in 2013. They have now sparked great enthusiasm around the world. Our roses come from Ecuador, which is known for high-quality and beautifully blooming roses. At this latitude the roses are grown and preserved under perfect climatic conditions.

A major advantage of Infinity roses is their ease of care. Unlike traditional roses, they do not require water or sunlight. On the contrary - it is better to keep them away from direct sunlight and moisture. A gentle dusting or blowing from time to time is usually enough to keep them looking fresh.

Infinity roses are more than just an eye-catcher. They are a symbol of lasting beauty and stability. They are often used as decoration in residential and commercial spaces or as gifts for special occasions such as weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, Mother's Day or Valentine's Day.

In summary, Infinity roses arranged in our rose boxes are a fascinating combination of nature and science. They offer an environmentally friendly and long-lasting alternative to traditional cut flowers. With their ease of care and durability, they are a perfect gift for lasting love and appreciation.
